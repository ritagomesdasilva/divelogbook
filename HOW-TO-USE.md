# 🤿 Dive Logbook — How to use

Welcome! This is a digital dive logbook. Nothing to install.

## 1. Get started (2 minutes)
1. Open the link you received in your phone's browser
2. **Tip:** tap "Share → Add to Home Screen" — it gets an icon, like an app
3. Tap **☁️ Sign in to sync** and enter your email
4. You'll receive an email with a **magic link** — open it **on the same device**. No passwords, that's it ✨

> You can also "Use without account", but your dives will stay on that device only. With an account, they live in the cloud and follow you everywhere.

## 2. Log a dive
- Tap the **＋** button (bottom-right corner on your phone)
- Fill in what you know — only the **site** is required
- Give your bubble rating 🫧 and tap **Save dive**

### Tricks
- 📷 **Photo of your paper logbook** → tap "✨ Fill fields from photo" and the form fills itself (always review before saving)
- 🏷️ **Photo of the dive centre stamp** → "✨ Read stamp" creates a digital version of the stamp
- ⌚ **Got a dive computer?** Menu ⋯ → "Import" accepts CSV, XML (Subsurface) and UDDF files. It shows what it found before adding anything, skips duplicates, and you can undo

## 3. Map
Your dive sites appear on the "Where I've dived" map. Tap **📍 Update map** (with internet) to locate new sites. If a site isn't found, add the island or country to its name (e.g. "Chumphon Pinnacle, Ko Tao").

## 4. FAQ
**Does it work offline?** Yes — log dives offline (on the boat, say) and they sync when you're back online.
**Who can see my dives?** Only you. Each account can only access its own records.
**New phone?** Sign in with the same email and your dives appear.
**Lost access to your email?** Your data is tied to your email — use menu ⋯ → "Save backup" now and then, just in case.

Happy bubbles! 🫧
